Surface Preparation
& Drywall Services

The foundation of durable, professional finishes

Back to Services

J&H Painting provides professional surface preparation and drywall services for commercial, residential, and new construction projects across Florida and nationwide. Proper preparation is the foundation of a durable and high-quality finish, and our team follows structured prep processes to ensure surfaces are properly repaired, primed, and ready for coating application.

We work in coordination with general contractors, builders, and property managers to prepare surfaces efficiently while maintaining jobsite cleanliness, safety, and schedule alignment.

Scope Includes:

  • Drywall repairs, patching, and finishing
  • Surface sanding, skim coating, and smoothing
  • Caulking, sealing, and joint preparation
  • Minor wall and ceiling repairs
  • Surface cleaning and coating removal
  • Priming and substrate preparation
  • Prep for repaint, renovation, and new construction projects
  • Punch list repairs and final surface corrections
Detailed Surface Preparation

Preparation Process & Quality Standards

Quality-Focused Prep Process

J&H Painting follows a detailed preparation process that may include surface inspection, patching, sanding, caulking, priming, and final surface conditioning before any paint or coating application. This ensures proper adhesion, uniform finishes, and long-term durability across interior and exterior surfaces.

Our team understands that high-quality finishes begin with proper preparation, especially on large-scale commercial, multi-unit, and new construction projects.

Jobsite Coordination & Efficiency

We are experienced in working within active construction sites, occupied facilities, and renovation environments. Our crews coordinate with project managers and superintendents to complete preparation work in phases, supporting project timelines, inspections, and final turnovers.
